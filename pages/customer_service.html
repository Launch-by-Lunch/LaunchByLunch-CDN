Our support team is available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time (EST).

We encourage all members to use the community forum when appropriate, as other founders and experts may have useful insights or similar experiences to share.

Thanks for being part of the Launch by Lunch community—we're here to help you thrive.
