Privacy Policy

Launch by Lunch LLC

Launch by Lunch LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates an AI Startup Community hosted on Circle.so. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you join our community.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you sign up:

  • Name and email address for account setup and communication
  • Billing information (such as credit card details) for processing payments
  • Profile details you choose to share within the community
  • Usage data, such as activity within the Circle.so platform, to help us improve the experience

All payment transactions are securely processed by Stripe, and we do not store your full payment details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide access to our community, resources, and content
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Communicate with you about updates, offers, or support
  • Monitor usage and improve our platform experience

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

Data Sharing

Your data is shared with:

  • Circle.so, our community platform provider, to power the experience
  • Stripe, our payment processor, to securely handle billing

Both vendors are GDPR- and CCPA-compliant.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your membership is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. Contact us to make a request.

Security

We take data security seriously. All data is stored using secure methods provided by our vendors, and payment information is encrypted via Stripe.
